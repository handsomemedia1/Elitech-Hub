What We Expect from Mentors

  • Dedicated Time Commitment

    Minimum 4-6 hours per month for mentorship sessions and guidance calls.

  • Career Guidance & Advice

    Help mentees navigate career decisions, certifications, and professional development.

  • Project Review & Feedback

    Review lab assignments and provide constructive, actionable feedback.

  • Professionalism & Reliability

    Maintain clear communication and uphold Elitech Hub's values.

  • Encouragement & Motivation

    Be a source of inspiration and help mentees build confidence.

Mentor Requirements

To ensure quality mentorship, we look for:

Experience

Minimum 3+ years hands-on experience in cybersecurity.

Education

Degree in CS/IT or equivalent experience. Certifications (CEH, CISSP, Security+) are a plus.

Communication

Excellent verbal/written skills. Ability to explain complex concepts simply.

Availability

At least 4-6 hours per month over a 3-month cycle.
