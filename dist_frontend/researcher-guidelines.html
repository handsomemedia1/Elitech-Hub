Researcher Guidelines

Everything you need to know about submitting and publishing research papers on Elitech Hub

Getting Started

Welcome to the Elitech Hub Research Platform! We're excited to have you contribute to our growing body of cybersecurity, AI, and technology research.

New to the platform? Start by creating your researcher account at the Researcher Portal. Registration is free and takes less than 2 minutes.

Account Requirements

  • Valid email address (institutional email preferred but not required)
  • Full name as it should appear on publications
  • Affiliation (university, company, or independent)
  • ORCID (optional but recommended for citation tracking)
  • Primary research area selection

Research Categories

We accept research papers in the following categories:

Cybersecurity
Cyberpsychology
Security Engineering
Artificial Intelligence
Machine Learning
Data Science
Network Security
Digital Forensics
Cryptography
Software Engineering

Submission Requirements

Paper Format

  • File Type: PDF format (required)
  • Maximum Size: 10MB
  • Length: 4,000 - 15,000 words (excluding references)
  • Language: English
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt, 1.5 line spacing

Required Sections

  1. Abstract (150-300 words) - Summary of your research
  2. Introduction - Background and research objectives
  3. Literature Review - Existing research and gaps
  4. Methodology - How the research was conducted
  5. Results/Findings - What you discovered
  6. Discussion - Interpretation and implications
  7. Conclusion - Summary and future work
  8. References - APA or IEEE format

Review Process

All submissions go through a rigorous peer review process:

1

Initial Screening (1-2 days)

Editor checks format, scope, and plagiarism

2

Peer Review (5-10 days)

2-3 expert reviewers evaluate the paper

3

Decision & Feedback

Accept, revise, or reject with detailed feedback

4

Publication

Approved papers are published and indexed

Quality Standards

What We Look For

  • Originality: Novel contributions to the field
  • Rigor: Sound methodology and analysis
  • Clarity: Well-written and organized
  • Relevance: Addresses current industry challenges
  • Impact: Potential practical applications

Common Rejection Reasons

  • High plagiarism score (>20%)
  • Off-topic or outside accepted categories
  • Poor writing quality or grammar
  • Inadequate methodology explanation
  • Missing required sections
  • Unsubstantiated claims without evidence

Strike Policy

To maintain platform integrity, we enforce a strike system:

  • Strike 1: Warning (plagiarism, policy violation)
  • Strike 2: 30-day submission suspension
  • Strike 3: Permanent account ban

Strikes can be issued for:

  • Submitting plagiarized content
  • Fraudulent author attribution
  • Manipulating citation metrics
  • Submitting duplicate papers
  • Violating ethical guidelines
