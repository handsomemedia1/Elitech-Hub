Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and services.

How do I enroll in a program?

You can enroll directly through our website by clicking the "Apply Now" button or visiting the Programs page. Alternatively, contact us via WhatsApp or email, and our admissions team will guide you through the enrollment process.

Do you offer corporate training for teams?

Yes! We provide customized corporate training programs tailored to your organization's specific needs. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we'll create a training plan that fits your team size, skill level, and business objectives.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and online payments. For the 16-week program, we offer flexible payment plans allowing you to pay in 2-3 installments. Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your budget.

When does the next cohort start?

We run multiple cohorts throughout the year. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to learn about upcoming start dates and reserve your spot in the next available cohort.
